# ANALSYS-OF-CUSTOMER-BEHAVIOUR-ON-FOOD-DELIVERY-BY-ONLINE
This project analyzes customer behavior in online food delivery platforms by studying ordering patterns, preferences, and spending habits. It explores factors like delivery time, pricing, and user experience to understand what drives customer choices and satisfaction, helping improve services and engagement.

**Project Description: Analysis of Customer Behaviour on Online Food Delivery**

In today’s fast-paced digital world, online food delivery platforms have become a part of everyday life. This project focuses on understanding how customers interact with these platforms — what influences their choices, how often they order, and what factors impact their satisfaction.

By analyzing customer data such as ordering patterns, preferences, spending habits, and feedback, this study aims to uncover meaningful insights into user behavior. It explores key questions like why customers prefer certain apps, what drives repeat orders, and how pricing, delivery time, and user experience affect decision-making.

The goal of this project is not just to work with data, but to tell a story about customers — their needs, expectations, and habits. These insights can help businesses improve their services, enhance customer experience, and build stronger engagement strategies.
